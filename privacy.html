Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This privacy policy explains how Socapex Tester Fabrication and Design Inc. ("we", "us") handles personal information collected through the Socopex Tester Box order form. We take your privacy seriously and only collect what we absolutely need to fulfill your order.

What We Collect

When you submit an order, we collect:

  • Your name
  • A contact method (phone number or email address)
  • Your order preferences (color choices and any optional custom text)

We do not collect payment information, location data, device identifiers, or any other personal data beyond what is listed above.

Why We Collect It

Your information is collected for one purpose only: to fulfill your custom order. Specifically, we use it to confirm your order details and to contact you when your item is ready.

How It Is Stored

Order information is recorded in a private Google Sheet accessible only to Socapex Tester Fabrication and Design Inc. An email notification containing your order details is sent to the order manager at the time of submission. No information is stored in any public database, third-party marketing platform, or advertising system.

Deletion

Once your order has been completed and delivered, your personal information (name and contact details) is deleted from our records. Order preference data (colors, text) may be retained in anonymized form for our own production records, but it will not be linked to you or your contact information in any way.

No Selling or Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with any third party for any purpose — commercial or otherwise. Your information never leaves our internal order management process.

Third-Party Services

Order submissions are processed using two third-party services:

  • Formspree — delivers a notification email to us. Formspree receives your form data momentarily in order to send that email. You can review their privacy practices at formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Google Sheets — stores your order details in a private spreadsheet. Google's privacy policy applies to data stored on their platform and can be found at policies.google.com/privacy.

Neither service is authorized to use your data for any purpose other than facilitating the transmission or storage of your order.

Your Rights

You may request that your personal information be deleted at any time — even before your order is complete. Just reach out to us and we will confirm deletion promptly.

Contact

If you have any questions about this policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us directly.
